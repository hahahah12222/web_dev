Honeybees

Honeybees are incredible insects that play a vital role in our ecosystem. They are social creatures, living together in large, organized colonies, typically inside hives or tree cavities. Each colony consists of three types of bees: a single queen, many female worker bees, and a few male drones. The workers are responsible for most of the colony's tasks, including foraging for nectar and pollen, building the honeycomb, and caring for the young, while the queen's primary job is reproduction.
