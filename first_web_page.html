Dirty Dancing

Plot

Frances "Baby" Houseman, a 17-year-old who has a summer romance with Johnny Castle, a poor dance instructor at the hotel where her family is vacationing in 1963. Baby becomes Johnny's dance partner after his original partner, Penny, is having a baby and decieds to have an abortion, and they fall in love. leading to conflict with Baby's father and Johnny's return to perform a final dance with Baby.
